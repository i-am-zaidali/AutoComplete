Dawood University of Engineering & Technology (DUET), Karachi, is a public sector institution located in Karachi, Pakistan. It is one of the notable engineering universities in the country, known for its quality education in engineering and technology fields. Here are the key highlights about DUET:
Establishment: DUET was established in 1962 under the Dawood Foundation, founded by Seth Ahmed Dawood, a prominent philanthropist and industrialist.
Public Sector: It was nationalized in 1971 and is now operated under the Government of Sindh.
Charter: The university received its charter in 2010, officially recognizing it as a degree-awarding institution.
DUET offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and related disciplines. Major programs include:
Faculty of Computer Science, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Computer System Engineering, Industrial Engineering & Management, Telecommunication Engineering, Petroleum & Gas Engineering
